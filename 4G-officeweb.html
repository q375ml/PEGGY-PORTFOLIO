Official Website Revision

UX Research・UI System Building・Prototype

Project Details

What I did －

  • Created end-to-end UX/UI design process from ideating a vision and goal to creating solutions and implementation.
  • Collaborate with different stakeholders, product manger, engineer to identify problems and provide solutions.
  • Delivered use cases, wireframes, high-fidelity interactive prototypes, specs, and annotations for onsite and remote dev teams.
  • The data collected internally and externally is used in the design, and usability testing and iteration are carried out after the launch.

Outcome －

  • 20% up

    Total website traffic

  • 70% up

    New users

  • 50% up

    Website interactivity

Goal －

  • Create community mechanism gamification experience and content interactivity.
  • Increase the number of user members on the official website.
  • Personalized service settings.
  • Improve interactive features and rewards to increase member engagement.
  • The website is more active to form an ecological circle.

Challenge －

  • Build team trust in the product.
  • Consistency of experience across devices.
  • The more intuitive reading interface.
  • Enhance the commercial value of each page.

The Problem －

Build trust by conducting interviews with stakeholders to understand the goals of internal users.

  • Single delivery of information, lack of interaction with members.
  • Enhance the business value of the layout.
  • A more comfortable and intuitive way to read, the current website lacks focus.
  • The outline of members is blurred, and the number of members cannot be increased.
  • Ads interfere with reading, and users do not know that membership has other purposes.

Role －

Senior UI/UX designer.

  • Conducting interviews.
  • Finding user insight.
  • Paper and digital wire-framing.
  • Low and high-fidelity prototyping.
  • Conducting usability studies and accounting for accessibility, iterating on designs.
  • Determining information building, and responsive design
